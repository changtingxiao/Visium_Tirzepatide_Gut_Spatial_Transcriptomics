Cell-type markers
Enterocytes: Apoa1, Apoa4, Alpi, Vil1
Fibroblasts: Acta2
Endothelial cells: Cd31 (for blood and lymphatic vessels), Pdpn (for lymphatic vessel)
Enteroendocrine cells: Chga, Cldn4
Neurons: Rbfox3 (NeuN), Tubb3 (Tuj1), Uchl1 (PGP9.5)
Goblet cells: Muc2, Ido1, Clca1
Tuft cells: Dclk1, Tuft2, Pou2f3
